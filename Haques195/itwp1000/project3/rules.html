Submission Guidelines

Welcome to the Online Photo Club! To maintain fairness and consistency, all submissions must adhere to the following rules:

Image Specifications

All submitted photos must be in a 1:1 aspect ratio (square). Images that are not square will be cropped automatically during the upload preview. We recommend uploading photos that are already in the correct aspect ratio to ensure your composition remains intact.

Accepted file formats are JPG, PNG, and GIF. The maximum file size allowed is 1MB. Larger files will be rejected during submission.

Themed Categories

All photos should align with one of our official categories: **Nature**, **Wildlife**, or **Still Life**. Submissions that do not fit these themes may be disqualified. Get creative and showcase your talent within these categories!

Content Guidelines

Submitted photos must be original work and free of offensive or inappropriate content. The Online Photo Club reserves the right to reject any photos that do not meet these standards.
