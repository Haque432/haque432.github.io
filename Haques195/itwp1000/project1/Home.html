About Warhammer 40K

Warhammer 40K is a tabletop miniature wargame set in a dystopian future where humanity battles various alien species and chaotic forces for survival. The universe is defined by perpetual war, gothic themes, and a brutal landscape where heroes, villains, and gods collide in epic confrontations. Developed by Games Workshop, Warhammer 40K has grown into a cultural phenomenon since its inception in the 1980s. Fans are captivated by its intricate lore, model-building aspects, and strategic gameplay. On this site, you’ll find an overview of some of the major factions, important battles, and tips on how to start exploring this complex, dark universe.

Whether you are drawn to the noble yet flawed Space Marines or the terrifying Chaos forces, there’s a faction for every taste in the Warhammer 40K universe. This website provides a beginner-friendly guide to the Warhammer 40K world, covering the vast array of factions, their unique strengths and weaknesses, and how each one fits into the larger narrative. Whether you're a long-time fan or completely new to the game, this site will guide you through the basics and beyond.
